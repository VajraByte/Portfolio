## Typography

### Fonts
- **Headlines**: Geist Bold (for brand impact)
- **Body**: Geist Regular (for clarity and readability)
- **Code**: Geist Mono (for technical elements)

### Font Weights
- Headlines: 700 (Bold)
- Subheadings: 600 (Semibold)
- Body: 400 (Regular)
- Small text: 500 (Medium)
